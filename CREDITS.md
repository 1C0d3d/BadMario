# Grand Reopening credits

## Game

**Grand Reopening: Last Light + Live Wire**

- Design, story adaptation, code, procedural art direction, level content, UI, and procedural audio: original to this project.
- Last Light canon premise and characters: the supplied *Grand Reopening - Game Narrative & Story Brief*.
- Multiplayer canon, public-reopening premise, six-zone route, mandatory co-op systems, and dialogue anchors: the supplied *Grand Reopening: Live Wire - Multiplayer Story Brief*.
- Engine: Godot Engine 4.7.

## Asset policy

No downloaded art, animation, font, music, or sound-effect packs are bundled. Characters, park environments, UI decorations, particles, and backgrounds are drawn at runtime with Godot primitives. Music and sound are synthesized at runtime by `AudioManager`.

Optional dialogue speech is synthesized locally by the player's operating-system text-to-speech service through Godot's `DisplayServer`. No voice model, recorded performance, or third-party voice asset is bundled.

If future contributors add external assets, they must record the creator, source URL, exact license, version/date, and required attribution here before committing the asset. Prefer CC0, MIT, OFL, or similarly permissive licenses. Do not accept unclear, noncommercial, branded-character, or ripped assets.
